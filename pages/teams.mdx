## Get started with PipeOps Teams

The PipeOps Teams feature enables developers to efficiently manage teams and their members. Follow the steps below to get started:

1. Click on your profile in the navigation bar. A dropdown menu will appear.
2. Select "Teams" from the dropdown menu. This action will redirect you to the teams page, where you can access all available teams.

Here's an example of the teams view page on PipeOps:

![Guide image](/assets/team/team_view1.png)

1. To view the members of a specific team, click on the desired team.
2. An image below illustrates the members in a specific team:

![Guide image](/assets/team/team_view2.png)

1. Creating a new team is easy. Just click on the "Create New Team" button located on the teams page. A convenient form will pop up, requesting the necessary information for the new team creation. Make sure to specify the access level of the team, such as project and cluster access.

Refer to the images below, which showcase the create new team form pop-up that you will encounter when creating a new team:
![Guide image](/assets/team/create_team.png)

![Guide image](/assets/team/select_team_cluster.png)

![Guide image](/assets/team/select_team_project.png)

1. Adding a team member to a specific team is a breeze. Navigate to the desired team and click on the "Add Team Member" button. A user-friendly form will appear, prompting you to provide essential information about the new team member. This includes their email address, admin privileges, and project/cluster access specifications.

Take a look at the image below, which displays the add new team member form pop-up that you'll see when adding a new team member:

![Guide image](/assets/team/add_member.png)

Now you're all set to efficiently manage your teams and team members using PipeOps Teams! Enjoy the seamless collaboration and enhanced development experience.
