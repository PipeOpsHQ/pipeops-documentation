Welcome to the PipeOps for developers documentation! This guide is designed to help developers optimize their workflows for simplicity and get started quickly without worrying about infrastructure. With PipeOps, you have the flexibility to customize your infrastructure as needed in the future.

## Developer Plan Features
The developer plan offers a variety of features to enhance your development experience. These include:

- Deployment of up to 10 projects
- Up to 5 Concurrent Builds
- Advanced Event Logs
- Automated CI/CD Pipelines

For more details on pricing and additional features, please visit our [pricing page](https://pipeops.io/pricing).
