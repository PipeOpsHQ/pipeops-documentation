# Overview

PipeOps for developers is designed to help developers optimize their workflows for simplicity and get started quickly without worrying about infrastructure. With PipeOps, you have the flexibility to customize your infrastructure as needed in the future.

## Developer Plan Features
The developer plan offers a variety of features to enhance your development experience. These include:

- Deployment of up to 10 projects: Developers can deploy and manage multiple projects simultaneously, up to a maximum of 10 projects.
- Up to 5 Concurrent Builds: PipeOps allows for up to 5 concurrent builds, enabling developers to run multiple builds simultaneously, saving time and increasing efficiency.
- Advanced Event Logs: Detailed event logs provide developers with insights into the status and progress of their projects, making it easier to track and troubleshoot issues.
- Automated CI/CD Pipelines: PipeOps supports automated CI/CD (Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment) pipelines, streamlining the process of building, testing, and deploying applications.

For more details on pricing and additional features, please visit our [pricing page](https://pipeops.io/pricing).
