PipeOps allows you to easily connect to and create any database of your choice, enabling you to store, manage, and analyze your data in a way that best fits your needs. With PipeOps, you can also deploy your database in the cloud or on-premises, giving you the flexibility to choose the deployment option that best suits your business requirements. PipeOps offers a range of powerful tools and features to help you build and manage your database, including data import and export capabilities, query optimization, and real-time monitoring. Whether you are looking to create a simple database for small-scale data storage or a complex database for large-scale data management, PipeOps has the tools and resources you need to get the job done.

To create/configure the database of choice, you can click the "AddOns" tab of your project and click on the "Check Them Out" Button.

To deploy a database using PipeOps, you can follow these steps:

1. From the list of available databases, choose the database that you want to deploy.
2. In the form, select the deployment method of your choice. 
3. Follow the prompts and instructions provided by the form to complete the deployment process. 
4. Once you have filled out all the required fields and made any necessary selections, click the "Deploy Database" button to initiate the deployment.
5. Wait while PipeOps processes your request and deploys the database. You should see a confirmation notification when the process is complete.
6. You can now access the deployed database using the provided connection details or credentials. You can also manage and monitor it from the PipeOps dashboard or other tools as needed.
