PipeOps for Startups is designed to help startups optimize their workflows for simplicity and get started quickly without worrying about infrastructure. With PipeOps, you have the flexibility to customize your infrastructure as needed in the future.

## Startups Plan Features
The startup plan offers a variety of features to enhance your development experience. These include:

- Deployment of up to 100 projects: Startups can deploy and manage multiple projects simultaneously, up to a maximum of 100 projects.
- Unlimited Concurrent Builds: PipeOps allows for unlimited concurrent builds, enabling startups to run multiple builds simultaneously, saving time and increasing efficiency.
- Teams for collaboration: Startups can create teams and collaborate seamlessly on projects within PipeOps, facilitating effective teamwork and project management.
- Deployment of up to 5 Servers: PipeOps supports the deployment of up to 5 servers, allowing startups to scale their infrastructure according to their needs.
- Automated CI/CD Pipelines: PipeOps supports automated CI/CD (Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment) pipelines, streamlining the process of building, testing, and deploying applications.
- Project and Cluster Metrics: Startups can access comprehensive metrics and analytics for their projects and clusters, gaining valuable insights into performance and resource utilization.

For more details on pricing and additional features, please visit our [pricing page](https://pipeops.io/pricing).
