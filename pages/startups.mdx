Welcome to the PipeOps for Startups documentation! This guide is designed to help startups optimize their workflows for simplicity and get started quickly without worrying about infrastructure. With PipeOps, you have the flexibility to customize your infrastructure as needed in the future.

## Startups Plan Features
The startup plan offers a variety of features to enhance your development experience. These include:

- Deployment of up to 100 projects
- Unlimited Concurrent Builds
- Teams for collaboration 
- Deployment of up to 5 Servers
- Automated CI/CD Pipelines
- Project and Cluster Metrics 

For more details on pricing and additional features, please visit our [pricing page](https://pipeops.io/pricing).

## Getting Started

This Getting Started guide will walk you through the process of deploying your first cluster and application on PipeOps. You can complete this guide in under 5 minutes.
