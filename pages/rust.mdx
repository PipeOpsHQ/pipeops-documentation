## Rust On PipeOps

PipeOps is the ideal platform for deploying your Rust applications due to its robust support. As you read on, you'll discover just how effortless it is to deploy your project.

## Getting Started

For a quick and hassle-free start, you can clone our pre-built Rust template from [here](https://github.com/pipeops-dev/pipeops-rust). This template comes with a Dockerfile that will be used during deployment. However, if you already have a project to deploy, you'll need to create a Dockerfile and copy the relevant information from our Rust template Dockerfile into your project's Dockerfile.

## Deploy Your Project

We assume that you've gone through our get started guide. If not, you can access it [here](https://docs.pipeops.io/developers). Once you've gone through the guide, you can proceed to connect your PipeOps account to your preferred git provider and select your project. In the image below, you'll find the necessary configurations for your Rust project. After completing these configurations, you can click on the "Deploy Project" button to deploy your project.
